Jessica Bottali

Who What Where

Impermanence

Photography

This project was a study of time. Initially coming into fruition after exploring the ghost town, Bodie, I grew increasingly fond of the delicate elegance and stillness in wear and weathering. Perhaps enamored. There is also a book of this work floating amid the web. Should you find it (please don’t)… Well, let’s just say I know you too have pretentious manuscripts hidden away from your college days. I won’t tell.
