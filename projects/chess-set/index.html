Chess Set

Product Design, Photography, 3D printing

Learning Solidworks was one of the first steps toward realizing my utter delight and regard for design. I’m terribly curious about how things are made, and this application helped me actualize my designs in tactile form. The knight from my chess set, is my first 3D print. The process was as exciting as waiting for a roll of film to develop, eagerly anticipating what will come through on your negatives. If you haven’t yet tried it, let me tell you, it’s addicting.
