Photography

Food photography feels a lot like portraiture for me because each leafy green or crisp apple seems to take on a it’s own character, and I find myself debating over which side of a Bartlett is it’s best side. There’s a relationship created that you just can’t find in other product work. Albert and Eve is drawing that connection even closer by bringing fresh, organic, local produce right to your door. You’ll be able to find more of these images on their new site, albertandeve.com, soon!
